Sarah's Bakery

Welcome to Sarah’s bakery! Started in 2015, Sarah’s bakery is a long-standing childhood dream and hobby turned into reality. From baking cakes as my favourite playtime activity and burning a few along the way, We have come a long way in trying to perfect the art.

We, at Sarah’s bakery, are here to make your birthdays, anniversaries, farewells, and any other festivity a tad more special and unique. Relish and dive into our luscious cakes, pastries, breads and cookies that are custom-made to fit your own unique needs/occasions. We strive to give you the best quality in every bite.
